About

I am a PhD researcher in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Roorkee. My research focuses on recommendation systems for software ecosystems, with an emphasis on project–library recommendation and higher-order modeling using graphs and hypergraphs.

Previously, I worked as a Junior Research Fellow at Jawaharlal Nehru University on a DBT-funded project on computer-aided diagnosis for intracranial atherosclerotic diseases using CT angiography.

Research

  • Third-party library recommendation and software ecosystem mining
  • Graph and hypergraph learning for recommendation
  • Robust similarity measures and evaluation protocols
  • Applied machine learning for medical imaging
